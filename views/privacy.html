Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 2024

1. Introduction

Devai Automation ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website youautomation.com, use our services, or interact with our platform.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, business details when you sign up or submit leads/forms.
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent (via cookies/analytics).
  • Lead Data: Customer names, phones, services from forms (processed for tenants).
  • Communication Data: Messages, conversations via integrated channels (Meta, SMS, etc.).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our SaaS automation platform (dashboards, lead management, AI replies).
  • Process leads and customer interactions for tenants.
  • Send service-related emails/notifications.
  • Analyze usage for better features (aggregated/anonymized).
  • Comply with legal obligations.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your data. We share with:

  • Service providers (Supabase DB, Twilio SMS, Meta APIs – under strict agreements).
  • Tenant users (lead data belongs to account owners).
  • Legal authorities if required.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies for functionality and analytics cookies (Google Analytics if enabled). You can manage via browser settings. No third-party ad cookies.

6. Data Security

We use encryption (Supabase), access controls, regular audits. However, no system is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You have rights to access, correct, delete your data (contact us). For EU users: GDPR compliant.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect their data.
